Retina Tomography Workbench

Two linked 3D views of GeneLab AWG retina micro-tomography: the whole eye anatomy on the left and toggleable segmented tissue layers on the right.

Controls

  • Rotate drag · Pan right-drag · Zoom scroll
  • Sync links rotation of both views
  • Rendering switches Surface / Wireframe / Slices
  • Slices cuts the meshes with three orthogonal planes (tri-planar MPR) to reveal interior structure

Data

Heavy source scans are decimated and Draco-compressed, cached in your browser, and stream with a progress bar. The Hugging Face dataset is unchanged.
