Retina Tomography Viewer

Two linked 3D views of GeneLab AWG retina micro-tomography data: the whole eye anatomy on the left and individually toggleable segmented tissue layers on the right.

Controls

  • Rotate — drag · Pan — right-drag · Zoom — scroll
  • Sync — link rotation of both views
  • Reset — re-frame a view, or both at once
  • Layers — toggle visibility, recolour via the swatch, fade with the slider

About the data

The source meshes are large scientific scans. Each downloads on demand, with a progress bar, and is cached in your browser so it only downloads once. Use Clear cached data to free that space.
